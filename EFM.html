What We Believe

Our Core Beliefs

Rooted in Scripture and centered on loving God and people. Our faith is built on the unchanging Word of God.

01 — The Bible
God's Inspired Word

The Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God — the ultimate guide for faith, life, and conduct.

2 Timothy 3:16
02 — God
The Holy Trinity

One God eternally existing in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — coequal, coeternal, Creator of all things.

Deuteronomy 6:4
03 — Jesus Christ
Son of God, Savior

Jesus is fully God and fully man. Born of a virgin, died for our sins, rose on the third day — the only way to salvation.

Acts 4:12
04 — The Holy Spirit
Empowering Presence

The Holy Spirit dwells within every believer, empowering holy living, bold witness, and spiritual gifts to build up the Body of Christ.

Acts 1:8
05 — Salvation
Grace Through Faith

Salvation is a free gift of God's grace, received by faith in Jesus Christ alone — not earned by works, but by His finished work on the cross.

Ephesians 2:8–9
06 — Bold Faith
Courageous Living

Faith calls us to live boldly — trusting God's promises, standing firm in truth, and acting in love and courage in every circumstance.

Hebrews 11:6

Next Steps

Water Baptism

If God has changed your life, baptism is your moment to declare it — boldly, publicly, and joyfully.

"We were therefore buried with Him through baptism into death in order that, just as Christ was raised from the dead... we too may live a new life."
Romans 6:4

Baptism does not save us — Jesus saves us — but baptism is a powerful step of obedience that follows salvation. We practice full immersion in water, following the example of Jesus.

✝️

Who Should Be Baptized?

Anyone who has accepted Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior, including new believers and those who wish to be baptized as a conscious decision of faith.

💧

What to Expect

A brief explanation and prayer, a personal declaration of faith, immersion in water in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, and celebration with the church family.

📝

How to Sign Up

Visit the Welcome Desk, register online, speak with a pastor or ministry leader, or attend a baptism class or orientation.

❓

Do I Need to Be Perfect?

No. Baptism is about surrender, not perfection. Bold faith often begins with a simple yes. We're here to walk every step with you.
